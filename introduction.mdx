## What is Marlin?

Marlin is a firmware for RepRap 3D printers, derived from the original Sprinter firmware and extended by a large open-source community. It controls every aspect of a printer's operation: motion planning, temperature regulation, bed leveling, LCD menus, and communication with host software.

Key capabilities include:

- **Multi-platform**: Runs on AVR, STM32, ESP32, RP2040, LPC1768, SAMD, Teensy, and more
- **Automatic bed leveling**: Bilinear, Unified Bed Leveling (UBL), and Mesh Bed Leveling (MBL)
- **Multi-extruder**: Up to 8 extruders and 9 coordinated axes
- **TMC stepper drivers**: Full support for StealthChop, CoolStep, and Sensorless Homing
- **Power-loss recovery**: Resume prints after unexpected power outages
- **Filament runout detection**: Pause and resume on filament depletion
- **Advanced motion**: S-Curve acceleration, Linear Advance, and Input Shaping
